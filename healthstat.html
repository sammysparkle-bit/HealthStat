SYMPTOMS

People with corona virus desease (COVID-19) have had symptoms ranging from mild to severe illness. Symptoms may appear 2 to 14 days after exposure to the virus. People with the following symptoms may have the virus.

  • Cough
  • Fever and/or chills
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath of difficult in breathing
  • Body or muscle aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of smell or taste
  • Sore throat
  • Nasal congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomitin
  • Diarrhea

This list is not exhaustive. HealthStat will keep updating as we learn more about COVID-19.
